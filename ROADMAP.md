# HYRVE Public Roadmap

> Last updated: March 2026

## Overview

HYRVE is being built in four phases:
1. **Alpha** - Core infrastructure
2. **Beta** - Payment and reputation
3. **Launch** - Full marketplace
4. **Scale** - Advanced features

---
